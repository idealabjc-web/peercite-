Who We Are

About PeerCite

Making world-class conference knowledge accessible to everyone since 2015

Our Mission

PeerCite Publishers bridges the gap between exclusive academic conferences and the global research community. We attend premier world conferences, collect speeches, keynotes, and research papers directly from speakers, and transform them into accessible, peer-reviewed journal articles.

Our goal is simple: the knowledge shared at the world's most important academic gatherings should not remain locked behind conference doors. We make it available to every researcher, student, and curious mind on the planet.

We curate content directly from conference speakers
Rigorous editorial and peer-review process
Open access — free for all readers worldwide
Indexed in Google Scholar, CrossRef & DOAJ
150+ Conferences Covered
