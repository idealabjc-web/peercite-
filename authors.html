Our Content

What We Publish

The types of conference content we curate and transform into journal articles

Keynote Speeches

  • Full speech transcripts from conference keynotes
  • Professionally edited for academic reading
  • Speaker-approved and fact-checked
  • Contextualized with supporting research
  • Attributed to the original speaker

Research Papers

  • Peer-reviewed papers presented at conferences
  • Original research with full methodology
  • Data sets and findings included
  • Properly cited and referenced
  • Indexed with DOI for citation

Panel Discussions

  • Expert panel discussion summaries
  • Multi-perspective academic debates
  • Curated consensus findings
  • All panelists credited and approved
  • Supplementary materials included
Quality Assurance

Our Editorial Standards

Every article published by PeerCite meets these rigorous criteria

Author Consent

All content is published only with the explicit consent and collaboration of the original Authors and researchers.

Peer Review

Every article undergoes review by at least two independent experts from our editorial board before publication.

Proper Attribution

Full credit is given to the original speaker, their institution, and the conference where the content was presented.

Open Access

All published content is freely accessible to anyone in the world — no paywalls, no subscriptions.
